About Us

Opened in 2010 with a dream to fill the world with childlike essence and sweets reimagined. Decorated from top to bottom with the most delicious, decadent candy ever seen. Eight rooms that will fill all you senses with sweet pleasures and make your mouth water. Easy on the eye decorations made from candy and created by our very own candy superstar Louis Alverex. We look forward to giving every candy lover who walks through our doors the special candy experience to create everlasting memories.

Why candy?

Why not? It brings smiles to every childs face and helps adults remember happy childhood memories. It's fun, creative, and a one of a kind experience. At the end of the tour there's even a candy store where you get to select some free candy, as well as purchase some more. Not only do we carry the every day convenience store candy, but we also carry exotic international candy. Candy from different regions and cultures that's hand made and spun with love.
